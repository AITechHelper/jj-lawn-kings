What We Do

Full-Service Lawn Care
for Tulsa Homeowners

From routine mowing to seasonal overseeding, J&J handles it all. Every job is done with care, consistency, and a commitment to leaving your yard looking its best.

🌿

Lawn Mowing & Maintenance

Weekly or bi-weekly mowing that keeps your lawn neat, healthy, and consistently trimmed. We edge, blow, and clean up after every visit so your yard always looks sharp.

🪨

Mulching

Fresh mulch improves moisture retention, suppresses weeds, and gives your flowerbeds a clean, finished look. We source quality material and spread it evenly for a polished result.

✂️

Hedge Trimming

Overgrown hedges can take away from an otherwise great yard. We shape and trim your bushes and hedges to keep them looking tidy and well-defined all season long.

🌱

Weeding

Weeds compete with your grass and plants for nutrients, water, and sunlight. We pull them out at the root and treat problem areas so they don't keep coming back.

🌾

Aeration & Overseeding

Compacted soil chokes your lawn's root system. Aeration opens it back up, and overseeding fills in thin or bare patches — resulting in a thicker, greener lawn over time.

🌸

Flowerbed Maintenance

We keep your flowerbeds clean, edged, and weed-free so your plants can shine. Whether it's a seasonal cleanup or ongoing upkeep, we treat your beds with the same care as the rest of your yard.

FAQ

Questions We Hear All the Time

Have a question not listed here? Give us a call or text anytime — we're happy to help.

How do I get a quote?▾
Just give us a call or text at (918) 510-1379, or fill out the form below. We'll get back to you quickly — usually the same day — with a straightforward, no-pressure quote.
Do you offer recurring weekly service?▾
Yes! Weekly maintenance is one of our most popular options. Customers love it because they never have to think about their lawn — we just handle it consistently every week.
What areas do you serve?▾
We serve Tulsa and the surrounding communities including Broken Arrow, Bixby, Jenks, Owasso, Sand Springs, Sapulpa, and more. Not sure if we cover your area? Just ask — we're happy to check.
Are you available on weekends?▾
We're open 24 hours, 7 days a week — including weekends. We work around your schedule to make lawn care as convenient as possible.
What's included in a standard mow?▾
Every mow includes cutting, edging along driveways and sidewalks, and blowing off clippings from hard surfaces. We leave your lawn looking clean and neat from every angle.
Do I need to be home when you come?▾
Nope! As long as we have access to the yard, you don't need to be home. Many of our regular customers aren't — we'll take care of everything and let you know when we're done.
When is the best time to aerate and overseed?▾
For most Oklahoma lawns, fall is the ideal time for aeration and overseeding — cooler temperatures help new seed establish before winter. We can assess your lawn and recommend the right timing for your grass type.
How do you handle pricing?▾
We believe in transparent, upfront pricing. We give you a clear quote before any work begins, and we stick to it. No surprise charges, no hidden fees — just honest work at a fair price.
